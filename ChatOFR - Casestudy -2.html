01 — The Problem

Researchers Were Waiting 45 Days to Do Their Job

The JADE platform was live, but adoption was stalling. Researchers were still emailing Excel files and using legacy tools. The bottleneck wasn't the platform — it was the onboarding.

🔒

Fragmented Provisioning

New researchers needed accounts across ServiceNow and Collibra — each with separate approval chains and manual enrollment steps.

⏱️

45-Day Wait Time

Manual provisioning across two systems meant researchers waited over a month before they could access data or begin analysis.

🏛️

Interagency Complexity

Each of the 5 agencies had different security classifications, governance rules, and bureaucratic processes.

📉

Adoption Stall

Despite launching a secure multi-agency platform, Q1 adoption was flat. Researchers defaulted to familiar, inefficient workflows.

04 — How We Got Here

From Discovery to Deployment

Week 1–2
Ride-Alongs & Discovery
Spent 2 weeks shadowing researchers across OFR, FRB, and FDIC. Discovered the 45-day onboarding bottleneck was the #1 reason for low adoption.
Week 3–4
Problem Reframing
Reframed the problem from "build a better platform" to "remove friction from getting started." Identified 2 systems that needed orchestration.
Week 5–8
MVP Design & Prototyping
Designed conversational interface using Claude Sonnet. Built API connectors to ServiceNow and Collibra. Implemented audit logging for federal compliance.
Week 9–10
Pilot & Validation
Ran pilot with 12 researchers. Measured onboarding time, error rates, and satisfaction. Iterated based on feedback.
Week 11–12
Launch & Scale
Rolled out to all 5 agencies. Onboarding dropped from 45 days to 12 days. Adoption tripled in the following quarter.
The hardest problems in government technology aren't technical — they're human. You have to build trust before you build software. ChatOFR proved that AI can be a bridge, not just a tool.

05 — Lessons

What I Learned

🎯

Trust is a Feature

In federal government, security and governance aren't blockers — they're user needs. We designed audit trails and data governance as first-class features, not afterthoughts.

🔍

Discovery Before Design

The 45-day onboarding problem was invisible until we did ride-alongs. Assumptions are expensive. Evidence is cheap. Always validate workflows before building.

🚀

Launch is the Beginning

JADE was technically successful but adoption was flat until we solved onboarding. The best product managers don't ship and walk away — they iterate based on real usage.

🤖

Agentic AI, Not Just Chat

ChatOFR isn't a chatbot — it's an agent that orchestrates actions across systems. The future of government UX isn't more dashboards; it's intelligent, conversational interfaces.
