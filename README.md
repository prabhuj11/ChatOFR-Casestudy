# ChatOFR Case Study

Two interactive, vibecoded write-ups of **ChatOFR**, an agentic AI system I designed to fix a 45-day researcher onboarding process across five federal agencies — cutting it down to a fraction of the time with zero tolerance for compliance failure.

## Why this exists

I'm Prabhu Jayasimha, an AI Product Manager who builds working proof-of-concepts instead of just writing specs. These pages are themselves an example of that: rather than a static slide deck, I "vibecoded" two fully interactive HTML case studies — no build step, no framework, just prompted, iterated, and shipped straight to the browser. It's the same instinct I bring to product work: get something real in front of people fast, then sharpen it.

## The short version

Federal researchers were waiting **45 days** just to start doing their job. ChatOFR is an agentic AI layer that orchestrates identity, access, and compliance workflows across disconnected agency systems — replacing a manual, error-prone process with one that's fast *and* auditable. Getting there required staging six nested pilots across five agencies, sequencing for risk rather than speed, and pushing back on pressure to launch before the system was ready.
